The Center for Law, History and Culture Invites all first year law students who are interested in the interdisciplinary study of law to join the "1L Metaphyiscal Club." Students who join the "metaphysical club" are invited to participate in the events sponsored by the Center for Law, History and Culture, including faculty workshops, conferences, symposia, and book talks. In addition to the club will meet at least twice a semester to discuss -- whatever we want! The metaphysical club also has an online discussion group.

To subscribe, follow these instructions:

1) send email to listproc@usc.edu
2) leave the subject blank
3) on the first line of text, put

subscribe Metaphysical-L firstname lastname

To send email to the listserv, address it to Metaphysical-L@usc.edu
